## 🌿 About Kauriflats

**Kauriflats** is a modern New Zealand marketplace built for people, businesses, landlords, sellers, service providers, tradies, and local communities.

Kauriflats is not just a property listing website. It is a growing **multi-category listing and business marketplace** where users can discover, list, promote, and manage different types of opportunities across Aotearoa New Zealand.

Visit the platform: **[kauriflats.co.nz](https://kauriflats.co.nz)**

## 🎯 Our Mission

Kauriflats exists to make online listings in New Zealand easier, smarter and more useful.

Our mission is to bring together people, property, products, services, vehicles, businesses and local opportunities into one clean, modern and trusted marketplace experience.

We want Kauriflats to become a powerful New Zealand platform for:

- 🏡 Rentals, flats and accommodation
- 🚗 Automotive and vehicle listings
- 🛒 Local products and marketplace items
- 🛠️ Tradies and service providers
- 🐾 Pets and animal-related listings
